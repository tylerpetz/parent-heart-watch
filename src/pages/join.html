BECOME A MEMBER

Your commitment to promoting Sudden Cardiac Arrest (SCA) awareness in your community is greatly needed. Parent Heart Watch has the knowledge, experience, information, and resources you need to bring SCA awareness to your own community and begin to change practices there.

Whether you are a parent who lost a child to SCA, have a child that has survived or is at risk for SCA, you are an SCA survivor, or you are a concerned individual or organization committed to prevention, joining Parent Heart Watch, the national organization solely dedicated to protecting youth from SCA, is the first step toward building a heart-safe community for all youth.

MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS INCLUDE:

  • Be represented at the national level
  • Network and unite with PHW members across the globe
  • Receive up-to-the-minute heart-related news stories, articles and research
  • Access information from top medical experts in the SCA in Youth field
  • Be involved in call to action initiatives that will positively impact and save young lives
  • Reduced registration fee to PHW Annual Conference
  • Child’s photo and story included on the PHW website
  • Receive monthly PHW e-newsletters
  • Access to sponsor or vendors offers available only to PHW members
