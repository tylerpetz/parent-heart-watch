MICHELE SNYDER

Executive Director

Michele@ParentHeartWatch.org

Michele Snyder has been Executive Director of Parent Heart Watch since September 2010. She has two decades of experience working with not-for-profit organizations in the areas of development, communications, media relations and event planning.Prior to working with Parent Heart Watch, sheowned and operated a consulting firm for non-profits for seven years. Her clients included SusanG. Komen for the Cure, the Chicago Children’sAdvocacy Center and the Junior League of Chicago. Having worked in radio, television and public relations throughout her career, Snyder boasts a strong media background. Her development experience encompasses strategic planning, researching and soliciting funding sources and partnership opportunities including individual, corporations and foundations. In 2008, Michele and her husband Bob lost their 17 year-old daughter Jenny to sudden cardiac arrest. Since that time, Michele has dedicated her life to protecting other families from this tragedy.

MAUREEN LEGG

Development & Communications Manager

Maureen@ParentHeartWatch.org

Maureen joined Parent Heart Watch as the Development and Communications Manager in April 2016. She brings extensive leadership,outreach, marketing and development expertise to the position. Prior to PHW, Maureen served as Executive Director of the Eric Paredes Save A Life Foundation, a PHW member foundation thatoffered youth heart screenings. While with EP Savea Life, she orchestrated a major rebranding, outreach and development campaign that nearly quadrupled sustainable income streams through grants, sponsorships and donor giving, doubled screening participation and created widespread awareness of Sudden cardiac arrest (SCA) prevention in youth through strategic partnerships and California legislation.
