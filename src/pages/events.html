12th Annual National Heart to Heart

January 13 – 15, 2017 | Tucson, Arizona
Omni Tucson National Resort

Do you want to learn more about saving young lives from SCA through education, heart screenings and emergency preparedness in case of a cardiac emergency? Are you looking for a community where you can belong, to share ideas and network?

Then you won’t want to miss this incredible opportunity! Join us at the 12th Annual Parent Heart Watch Heart to Heart in beautiful Tucson this January. Learn from experts, discuss heart screenings, AED placement, legislation and much more. You will leave empowered to make a difference in your community.
